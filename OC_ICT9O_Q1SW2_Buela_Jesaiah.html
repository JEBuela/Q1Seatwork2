CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Image Name of the Character Description
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin The well-written main character of the book. He is roled as the protagonist of the Noli Me Tangere book. Ibarra is a handsome young man with a brilliant mind.
Padre Damaso He is written as the symbolism of the evil. The anatagonist of the story of the Noli Me Tangere book. A priest filled with arrogance and selfishness. This character is against the main character, Crisostomo Ibarra.
Padre Salvi He is the town Curate of San Diego and the one who brought up Padre Damaso. People think of him as a person who's out of his mind.
Captain Tiago Kapitan Tiago was the owner of the house in the first chapter. He is good friends with Padre Damaso. He is the father of Maria Clara
Maria Clara The leading lady of Crisostomo Ibarra. She is the beautiful daughter of Captain Tiago. Her mother, Dona Pia, passed away giving birth to her and is unfortunately not present in the book.
